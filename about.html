about

I am an Indonesian research assistant in Advanced Photonics and Photovoltaics (APP) group at The Center fo Solar Energy Research and Applications (GUNAM) at Middle East Technical University (METU). I am now working towards my master of science degree in Micro and Nanotechnology program under the supervision of Asst. Prof. Selcuk Yerci. I graduated from Physics department of METU in 2014 with high honour degree. My main research evolves around the fabrication of thin crystalline silicon solar cells with advanced light trapping. My side projects include thin silicon peeling with stressor layer and liquid epitaxy of germanium on insulator.
